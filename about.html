About Me

I am a Software Engineer working at an educational games studio in the midwest. My work there is currently under NDA, but my current focus in my job is Unity and C# Game Development on multi-platform games.

I graduated from the University of Wisonsin-Madison B.S. Computer Science in May of 2019. During my time in College I was an officer in both the UW-Madison Game Design and Development Club and the Madison Student Chapter of The Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE). I learned a lot about the design and development process during this time and made multiple prototypes and fleshed some out into full games.

Besides my passion for game design, coding, and writing, other hobbies include competitive three-event and show-ski waterskiing, playing board games and D&D with friends, and taking care of my two corn snakes.
